Twisty Ride

This was my second game more brainstormed game. This wasn't that such of a mess program, because I actually started to get better and started to know really good the Unity game engine. This time I had a second co-worker working on this game. This game was rather speculative to make. At first we did not knew what we wanted from this game, but eventually we got the result what you see here in the screenshots.

The most challenging thing developing this game was constructing those levels. Those level took a very look time to make and it was rather annoying to make.

The thing what I'm most proud of is the mechanic of generating a level. I programmed that from scratch and it took a pretty long time. Fun fact, I used and reworked this mechanic in a another featured game. I was a nice this to remake.
